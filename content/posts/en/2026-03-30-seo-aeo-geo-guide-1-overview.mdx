Running a blog diligently but not showing up in search results? It might be time to check if you're only focusing on SEO while missing out on AEO and GEO. The 2026 search environment demands three optimization strategies simultaneously.

> Key Answer: SEO is for search rankings, AEO is for AI answer boxes, and GEO is for AI chatbot citations. For blog growth, applying them in the order of SEO → AEO → GEO is most efficient.

---

## What is SEO? The Basics of Search Engine Ranking

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is an optimization strategy to rank higher on search engines like Google and Naver. As of 2026, 53% of global web traffic comes from organic search (Source: BrightEdge, 2026).

### Core Elements of SEO

| Element | Description | Importance |
|---------|-------------|------------|
| Keyword Optimization | Placing target keywords in titles and content | Very High |
| Meta Tags | Optimizing title and description | High |
| Sitemap | Submitting page listings via sitemap.xml | High |
| Structured Data | Schema.org markup | Medium |
| Page Speed | Passing Core Web Vitals | High |

> Pro Tip: SEO has two pillars - technical setup (robots.txt, sitemap) and content quality. Technical setup is a one-time task, but content requires ongoing management.

---

## What is AEO? The Secret to AI Answer Box Exposure

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is a strategy to appear in "answer boxes" like Google AI Overview and Naver AI summaries. As of 2026, approximately 40% of Google searches display AI Overview (Source: Search Engine Land, 2026).

### Why AEO Matters

Traditional search had a "click one out of 10 links" structure, but AI answer boxes show only one answer. Being selected here increases CTR (click-through rate) by an average of 2.5 times according to research.

> Key Answer: The core of AEO is the "question-form title + 50-100 character immediate answer in first paragraph" structure. Content must be written in a format that's easy for AI to extract answers from.

### 4 Core AEO Rules

1. Use question-form titles - "How do you use Claude AI?" format
2. Immediate answer in first paragraph - provide core answer in 50-100 characters
3. Question-form H2/H3 headings - "What are the main features?" format
4. Add FAQ section - include 2-3 frequently asked questions at the bottom

---

## What is GEO? Strategy to Get ChatGPT to Cite Your Content

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is a strategy to get generative AI like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity to cite our content when answering. According to Princeton University research, content with GEO application saw up to 40% increase in AI citation rates (Source: Princeton GEO Research, 2024).

### Core GEO Strategies

| Strategy | Effect | Difficulty |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Adding Statistics/Numbers | AI citation rate +30-40% | Easy |
| Source Attribution | Increased credibility score | Easy |
| llms.txt Distribution | Improved AI crawler understanding | Medium |
| Comparison Tables/Structured Data | Easy AI extraction | Medium |

> Key Point: GEO's core is creating "content that AI wants to cite." Specific numbers, reliable sources, and clear answer structures are essential.

## SEO·AEO·GEO Implementation Priority

While doing all three simultaneously is ideal, phased implementation is more efficient for resource-limited personal blogs.

1. Phase 1 - SEO Basic Setup (1-2 days): Register Google Search Console and Naver Search Advisor, submit sitemap, configure robots.txt
2. Phase 2 - AEO Content Structure (1 week): Apply question-form titles, immediate answer structure, FAQ sections
3. Phase 3 - GEO Expansion (ongoing): Deploy llms.txt, insert statistics, cite sources, strengthen structured data

> Key Answer: The recommended implementation order for AI blogs like seroai.xyz is SEO basic setup → AEO content structure → GEO expansion. You must solidify the foundational SEO first.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1. Can I do just one of SEO, AEO, or GEO?
A1. SEO is essential. AEO and GEO are additional strategies built on top of SEO, so their effectiveness is limited without SEO. We recommend applying at least SEO + AEO together.

### Q2. When should I start with GEO?
A2. Start after completing basic SEO and AEO setup. Since llms.txt deployment and statistics insertion can be applied immediately, making them habits during content creation requires no separate time investment.

### Q3. Are Naver and Google SEO different?
A3. The basic principles are the same, but Naver emphasizes blog dwell time and keyword matching more, while Google focuses more on E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust) and structured data.
