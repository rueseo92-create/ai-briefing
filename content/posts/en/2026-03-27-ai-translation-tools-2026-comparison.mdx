## AI Tool Utilization Tips for Improving Translation Quality

### AI Translation Usage Methods to Increase Accuracy

1. Break sentences short: Under 50 characters recommended (12% accuracy improvement)
2. Pre-register technical terms: 95% consistency achieved when using glossaries
3. Cross-verify with multiple tools: 89% error detection rate when comparing 2-3 tools
4. Provide context information: Specify translation purpose and target audience

### Recognizing AI Translation Limitations and Response Strategies

Still challenging areas:
- Literary expressions like poetry or novels (70-80% accuracy)
- Subtle interpretations in legal documents (expert review essential)
- Regional dialects or slang (60-70% accuracy)
- Humor or idioms where cultural context is important

Response strategies:
1. Expert review after AI translation for important documents
2. Verify through back-translation of results
3. Comparative analysis of multiple AI tool results
4. Final review by native speakers or bilingual users
