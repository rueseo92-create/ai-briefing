## Practical Construction: Follow Along in 30 Minutes with Free Tools

### Creating Conversation Flow (15 minutes)

This is the most important part of customer service chatbot construction, but don't overthink it.

1. **Set Start Message**
   - "Hello! I'm the AI consultant for OO Company 😊"
   - Add 4 selection buttons: "Business Hours", "Pricing Inquiry", "Technical Support", "Other"

2. **Set Response for Each Button**
   - Business Hours: "We operate weekdays 9AM-6PM, Saturdays 9AM-1PM"
   - Pricing Inquiry: Price table image + "For detailed consultation, please call 02-0000-0000"
   - Technical Support: Connect to representative or provide FAQ link

3. **Set Fallback Message** (when can't understand)
   - "Sorry, let me connect you to a representative"

### Testing and Deployment (10 minutes)

The customer service chatbot deployment failure rate in 2026 is 12%, mostly due to insufficient testing. Be sure to check the pre-deployment checklist:

- [ ] Completed testing of 5+ major scenarios
- [ ] Completed typo and grammar check
- [ ] Confirmed normal operation on mobile screens
- [ ] Tested representative connection function

> Pro Tip: Ask family or colleagues to test first. You can find missing parts from a third-party perspective.
