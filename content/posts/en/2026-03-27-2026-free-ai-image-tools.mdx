## AI Image Generation Tool Usage - Complete Beginner's Guide

### Effective Prompt Writing Methods

Prompts are key to getting good AI image generation results. Refer to this structure:

1. Subject description: "Cute cat"
2. Style specification: "In watercolor style"
3. Color specification: "With warm tones"
4. Background description: "By a sunny window"
5. Quality keywords: "High quality, detailed"

Excellent prompt example:
"Watercolor style cute orange cat, napping by a sunny window, warm tones, high quality, detailed, 4K resolution"

### Optimal AI Image Generation Tool Selection Guide by Purpose

- Blog/SNS use: DALL-E 4 or Naver Clova

A: Yes, as of 2026, the quality of free tools has greatly improved, enabling generation of considerably professional-level images. Particularly, Adobe Firefly 3 and DALL-E 4 free versions show results comparable to paid tools.

A: Naver Clova is most excellent in understanding Korean prompts. Next, DALL-E 4 and Kakao KoGPT understand Korean well and reflect Korean expressions and cultural elements effectively.

### Q3: What are the alternatives when generation limits are exceeded?
A: You can use multiple platforms alternately, or utilize open-source tools like Stable Diffusion XL 3.0 that allow unlimited use. Also, since limits reset monthly, planned usage is recommended.

A: While policies differ by platform, it's advisable to disclose AI-generated images for transparency and ethical use. Especially for commercial purposes or official announcements, disclosure is recommended.

A: Most tools provide basic editing features, and additional editing is possible in Photoshop or other image editing programs after download. DALL-E 4 and Adobe Firefly 3 have particularly excellent built-in editing features.

### Q6: Can they be used on mobile?
A: Naver Clova and Kakao KoGPT are well-optimized for mobile, and DALL-E 4 and Adobe Firefly 3 can also be used smoothly on mobile through web browsers.
